fifarosters.com Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how fifarosters.com ('we', 'our', or 'us') collects, uses, protects, and handles your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) while you interact with our website.

Personal Information Collection

When registering or ordering on our site, you may be asked to provide your name, email address, or other details. Additionally, for security purposes and to ensure a good user experience, we may log IP addresses and collect information about your device type and browser type.

Use of Information

We use your information in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience and deliver content and product offerings relevant to your interests.
  • To send periodic emails regarding your account or other site related services.

Protection of Information

Your personal information is contained behind secured networks. We implement various security measures when you enter, submit, or access your information to ensure its safety. All sensitive information you supply is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

We do not collect or store transactions or financial data on our servers.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your user data as long as your account is active. Upon deletion of your account, we delete your personal information from our database. To request the deletion of your account, please email us at [thomas at fifarosters dot com] from the email address associated with your account.

Note that some user-generated content may not be immediately removed upon account deletion, but this content does not contain personally identifiable information.

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Understand and save user's preferences for future visits.
  • Compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interactions to offer better site experiences and tools in the future.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your PII to outside parties. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Third-Party Links and Services

At our discretion, we may include or offer third-party products or services on our website. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content and activities.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to access and edit your information via your Account settings page. You also have the right to delete your account. All necessary consents are gathered via our Consent Management Platform.

Data Breach Procedure

Should a data breach occur, we will notify users via email within 7 business days and via in-site notification within 7 business days.
